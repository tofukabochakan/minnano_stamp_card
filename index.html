Privacy Policy

Minnano Stamp Card
Operator: Tofukabochakan

1. Information Collected

The App handles the following types of information:

Images stored on the user’s device
Users may select images from their device to use as stamp icons. These images are used only within the App and are never uploaded or transmitted externally.

Stamp card data
Titles, stamp counts, notes, image paths, and other stamp card information are stored locally on the user’s device.

Backup data
When the user performs a backup, the App generates a JSON file and saves it locally on the device. No backup data is sent to external servers.

Advertising-related data (Google AdMob)
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect information such as device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, in accordance with its own privacy policies.

2. Purpose of Data Use

The App uses data for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing stamp cards
  • Providing backup and restore functionality
  • Displaying advertisements through Google AdMob

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

3. Data Storage and Management

All data handled by the App is stored locally on the user’s device.
No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers.

When the App is uninstalled, all locally stored data is deleted from the device.

4. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share user data with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law
  • When Google AdMob processes data necessary for advertising, in accordance with Google’s policies

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for general audiences, including children and families.
The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Advertisements are displayed in accordance with applicable child safety and advertising policies.

6. Use of External Services

The App uses the following external service:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Compliance

This App does not sell personal information.

Users can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting their Google Ads settings:
https://www.google.com/settings/ads

8. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the App uses Google’s certified Consent Management Platform (CMP) to obtain user consent for personalized advertising in accordance with GDPR requirements.

Users can change their consent preferences at any time from the app settings.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary.
Any changes will be announced within the App or on the published policy page.
